Google will soon introduce searching for podcast episodes as part of its search results. This much was said during the keynote of this year’s Google I/O. At first, I thought that this might put down my efforts to build a podcast search engine myself, but then i thought it through. The fact that Google recognizes the importance of podcasts is not only not a motivation killer, but also a serious reassurance about their importance.

You can check how your podcast will look among Google’s search results: Podcast Preview
